English: The Iraq War (2003 - December 2011), also known as the Second Gulf War , was a military engagement encompassing the invasion and occupation of Iraq by a U.S. -led coalition and an asymmetrical war between an insurgency and coalition troops as well as the New Iraqi Army , as well as violence between the country's Shia and Sunni populations.
